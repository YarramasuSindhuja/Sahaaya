Coping Strategies

Mindfulness & Meditation

Practice mindfulness techniques to stay present and reduce stress. Try guided meditation sessions.

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Body scan meditation
  • Mindful walking
  • Progressive muscle relaxation

Physical Wellness

Regular exercise and physical activity can significantly improve mental health and mood.

  • Daily walks or runs
  • Yoga or stretching
  • Team sports
  • Dance or movement

Social Connection

Maintain healthy relationships and social connections to support your mental well-being.

  • Regular check-ins with friends
  • Join clubs or groups
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Family time
